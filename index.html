Sergio Ibarra-Espinosa

Bio

I study vehicular emissions and its impact on air quality. I develop R packages with Fortran interfaces make the calculations faster. I have worked with big data of GPS IoT to develop traffic flow. I developed the model VEIN to estimate vehicular emission. VEIN is used in many countries in the globe and includes emission factors for China, Europe, Brazil and United States. I collaborate with researches in air quality, mobility and climate change.
